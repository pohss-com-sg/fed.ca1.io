did you know?

  • Bencoolen MRT station is the deepest MRT station in Singapore as of 2026, with a depth of 43 metres.
  • The longest bus route in Singapore is 858, with a distance of 73.4 km and 74 stops!
  • Conversely, the shortest bus route in Singapore is 902, with a distance of 1.0 km and two stops.
  • The most used MRT station in Singapore is Jurong East, serving over 64 000 passengers per day!
  • Conversely, the least used MRT station is Hume, serving less than 2000 passengers per day.
  • There are four bus operators in Singapore.
    • The parent companies of GoAhead and Tower Transit are actually from the UK!
  • Teck Lee did not open for 20 years due to a lack of development around the area.
  • Articulated buses (endearingly called bendy buses) used to be more common, with a peak of 240 buses in 2014. Now, there are only 40 buses as of 2026. You can track them here.
